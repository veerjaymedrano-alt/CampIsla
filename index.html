Camp Isla is a peaceful place surrounded by clear water and rich natural beauty. The area receives recognition for its green tree coverage and fresh air and peaceful atmosphere. Students visit the island to study marine life and environmental conservation methods. The island features enjoyable activities that promote team building and friendship development. The beautiful scenery of the location creates an ideal environment for educational activities and peaceful time spent outside. Camp Island uses its natural beauty to teach visitors about environmental protection. The experience teaches students about the environment while creating lasting memories.

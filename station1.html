Classified IS-pecies

Our group observed and collected marine organisms while we identified them through their physical features and assigned them to specific Linnaean Classification System categories.

I have learned that scientists use taxonomy to categorize living organisms based on shared characteristics between different species. I learned that marine species which appear alike actually belong to distinct scientific classification systems. The activity demonstrated to me that careful species observation is needed for proper identification. The process of classification enables scientists to study every marine organism's ecological function which helps protect ocean biodiversity. I found that the ocean contains many different types of marine species which each occupy their own special ecological role.

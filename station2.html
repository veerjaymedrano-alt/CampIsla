Coastal IS-tewards

Our team performed environmental cleanup work at the coastal site to collect various types of garbage while studying the state of both the beach area and the mangrove forest.

I have learned that coastal pollution creates devastating effects that impact both marine organisms and their natural habitats. The research showed me that mangroves protect shorelines through their essential functions which prevent erosion and shield against powerful ocean waves. The activity demonstrated to me the ways which human behavior impacts the ecological well-being of coastal ecosystems. The research showed me that effective waste management systems work to decrease ocean pollution problems. The most important lesson I learned showed me that all people need to take responsibility for environmental protection work.

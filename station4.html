Bio IS-timulant

Our group constructed a basic bioremediation system which used gravel, sand, charcoal, and duckweed to purify a water sample. We conducted water quality tests before and after treatment to document the changes in water quality.

I learned that bioremediation uses living organisms to help clean polluted water. I discovered that plants together with filtration materials can naturally enhance water quality. The experiment demonstrated to me that pH and oxygen and turbidity levels undergo changes following treatment. The study showed me that statistical methods enable researchers to assess differences between results obtained before and after filtration. Nature provides solutions to environmental problems according to my most important discovery.

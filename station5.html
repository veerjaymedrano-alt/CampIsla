Aquatic Live IS-tocks

Our team went to an aquaculture facility to study the controlled farming methods used to raise crabs. The team documented their findings about the different aspects of feeding patterns and animal habitats and their growth patterns and water management practices.

I learned that aquaculture helps provide food while protecting natural fish populations. I discovered that proper feeding and clean water are important for the health of aquatic animals. This activity showed me how farmers carefully monitor the growth of crabs. Sustainable fish farming practices enable human development while preserving environmental resources according to my findings. The main discovery I made showed me.
